About Us

The IoT Hackathon is an innovation-driven competition where developers, engineers, and tech enthusiasts collaborate to build smart, connected solutions. Participants leverage sensors, microcontrollers, cloud platforms, and real-time data to develop groundbreaking applications in areas like smart cities, healthcare, automation, and more. With a focus on creativity and problem-solving, this hackathon encourages rapid prototyping, hands-on learning, and impactful solutions that push the boundaries of IoT technology.

Do You Have any questions about Hack With IoT?

Get to know what our audience is concerned about

Can I cancel my participation before Hack With IoT begins?

+
Cancellation is not allowed, but you can transfer your registration to another participant.

What is Hack With IoT 2025?

+
Hack With IoT 2025 is a technology and innovation event featuring hackathons, workshops, and networking opportunities for tech enthusiasts.

Who can participate in Hack With IoT?

+
Students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts from all backgrounds can participate.

What topics will be covered in the workshops?

+
Workshops will cover web development, AI, cybersecurity, blockchain, and other emerging tech trends.

What is the prize pool for the hackathon?

+
The hackathon offers cash prizes, internships, and goodies for winners.

Do I need a team to participate in the hackathon?

+
You can participate solo or in a team. Teams can be formed before or during the event.
